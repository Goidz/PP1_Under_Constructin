The Giddy Goat

 Gigs About

Founded on Groove & Rock'n'Roll

Born in the murky bowels of Neukölln, Berlin. We have for decades fed on the sound of crushing guitar amps, stomping drums and cold beer. Driven by a purpose to collectively push the boundries of all genres within Rock and Metal. To create an open and diverse community of like-minded humans. Having our stage graced by many, from Little Richard, to Motörhead, to Gojira and everything in between. With your support The Giddy Goat marches forth in a world ever less open to musicianship. So, come in from the cold, raise your horns and beer and support the multitute of tallent we are lucky to have play here.
